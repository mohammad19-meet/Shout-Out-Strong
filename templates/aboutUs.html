WHO ARE WE

We are a group of teenage entrepreneurs creating an organization that will mainly focus on raising awareness on sexusal assaults and creating a strong and united front that will help reduce the problem and hopefully ending it. Our goal is to make people think about their actions and reconsider their legitimacy. On this website we will have every piece of information about us and our organization, including lectures and our products.

VISION

We envision everyone in our region would feel safe, take ownership of their own body and feel secure that people would respect them.

MISION

Our movement aim is to create a strong and united front of people who want the sexual assault phenomena to end. We want to encourage victims to share their own stories and ask our supporters to participate in rallies, that would raise awareness to the subject.
